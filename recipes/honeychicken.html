Honey Garlic Chicken

Description

Honey garlic chicken is a delicious combination of sweet and savory flavors, featuring crispy chicken coated in a sticky, flavorful sauce. The richness of butter, the sharpness of garlic, and the sweetness of honey come together to create a balanced and satisfying dish.

This recipe is perfect for a quick dinner and pairs well with rice or vegetables. It’s easy to make and can be customized with extra spice or additional toppings like green onions or sesame seeds.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch

Steps

  1. Coat chicken pieces in cornstarch.
  2. Cook chicken in a pan until golden and crispy.
  3. Remove chicken and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, melt butter and add garlic.
  5. Add honey and soy sauce, then stir.
  6. Return chicken to the pan and coat evenly in sauce.
  7. Cook until sauce thickens, then serve.
